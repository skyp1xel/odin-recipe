Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

This recipe is from allrecipes website, Posted by sara. Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1 : Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step 2 : Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.
  3. Step 3 : Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese.
  4. Step 4 : Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  5. Step 5 : Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  6. Step 6 : Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese. Serve and enjoy!
